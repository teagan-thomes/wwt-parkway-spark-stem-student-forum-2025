Mastering Undo and Redo

Why It Matters

Undo and redo functions are essential for correcting mistakes and refining your work. They allow you to revert changes or reapply them, making your workflow more efficient and less stressful.

The Basics

  1. Undo: Revert the last action you performed.
  2. Redo: Reapply the action you just undid.

Keyboard Shortcuts

For Windows Users

  • Undo: Ctrl + Z
  • Redo: Ctrl + Y

For Mac Users

  • Undo: Command (⌘) + Z
  • Redo: Command (⌘) + Shift + Z
